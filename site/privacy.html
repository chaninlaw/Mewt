Mewt Mic
Menu-bar mic mute for macOS

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 18 June 2026

The short version: Mewt Mic collects nothing, sends nothing, and stores nothing about you on any server. The app runs entirely on your Mac. There is no account, no sign-in, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no network activity of any kind.

What this policy covers

This policy describes how the macOS application Mewt Mic ("the app", "we") handles information. It applies to the version distributed through the Mac App Store and to any future builds we publish.

Data we collect

None. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal information.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or location.
  • We do not collect usage analytics, telemetry, or feature-usage data.
  • We do not collect crash reports.
  • We do not require — or offer — an account, email address, or sign-in.

Microphone access

Mewt Mic requests access to your microphone for two reasons, both of which run entirely on your Mac:

  • System-wide mute — controlling the macOS input device so other apps cannot capture audio.
  • Input level display — reading a real-time amplitude value to draw the level meter and the "talking" mascot state.

Audio buffers are processed in memory only. They are never recorded, never written to disk, and never transmitted off your Mac. Mewt Mic cannot transcribe, recognise, or save what you say.

Network use

Mewt Mic does not make any network requests. The app does not contact our servers, third-party servers, advertising networks, analytics providers, or any other remote endpoint. You can use Mewt Mic with networking entirely disabled.

Data stored on your Mac

The app stores a small amount of configuration locally using macOS's standard User Defaults system. This stays on your Mac and is never uploaded. It includes:

  • Your chosen hotkey bindings.
  • UI preferences, such as whether the welcome card has already been dismissed.
  • Feature-flag state for in-app options.

This use of User Defaults is declared in the app's privacy manifest under the reason code CA92.1, as required by Apple.

Children's privacy

Mewt Mic does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe to use at any age.

Third-party services

Mewt Mic does not use third-party SDKs that collect data, third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or social-media trackers. The only third-party code in the app is the open-source Swift library KeyboardShortcuts, used to register the global hotkey. This library runs entirely on-device and performs no network activity.

Your rights

Because we never collect or store data about you, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, delete, or export. You remain in full control of your local preferences through the app's Settings… screen, and you can revoke microphone access at any time in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles information — for example, if a future version adds a feature that involves network use — we will update this page and bump the effective date above before that version ships. Material changes will be summarised in the app's release notes.
