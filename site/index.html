Mewt Mic
Menu-bar mic mute for macOS

Support

Mewt Mic is a tiny menu-bar utility that mutes your microphone system-wide and shows the input level at a glance. This page covers the most common questions. If you don't find what you need, get in touch below.

Quick start

  1. Launch Mewt Mic. A cat-face icon appears in the menu bar (top-right of the screen).
  2. Click the icon. On first launch, a welcome card explains what the app does and how the microphone permission is used.
  3. Click Grant Microphone Access and choose Allow in the system dialog.
  4. That's it. Use the Mute button, the hotkey, or right-click the menu-bar icon to toggle.

Default hotkeys

Toggle mute: ⌥ + M

Push to talk: hold ⌥ + Space (mic unmutes while held, re-mutes on release)

Both hotkeys can be customized in Settings… inside the popover.

FAQ

I don't see Mewt in the Dock.

That's by design. Mewt Mic is a menu-bar accessory app (LSUIElement) and only lives in the menu bar. Look for the cat-face icon in the top-right of the screen.

The mute doesn't seem to affect Zoom / Google Meet / Discord.

Mewt mutes at the operating-system level (CoreAudio input). Most meeting apps respect this — your voice will not be transmitted. Some apps display their own in-app mute state separately from the system input, so the app's own mute button may still show as "unmuted" even though no audio is captured. The input-level bar in Mewt is the source of truth: if it stays flat while you talk, you are muted.

I clicked "Don't Allow" on the microphone prompt.

You can re-enable access in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone and turn on Mewt Mic. Toggling the mute button via the menu-bar will still work, since that path uses a separate CoreAudio API that does not require permission. The input-level meter and the talking indicator require microphone access to function.

Does Mewt send any data anywhere?

No. The app makes no network requests, has no analytics, no crash reporting, no account, and no sign-in. Audio is processed locally and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. See the Privacy Policy.

How do I quit Mewt?

Right-click the menu-bar icon and choose Quit. Or use ⌘ + Q while the popover is open.

How do I uninstall?

Drag Mewt Mic from your Applications folder to the Trash. To clear the saved hotkey and preferences, you can also run defaults delete com.chaninlaw.Mewt in Terminal.

The hotkey doesn't work.

Two common causes:

  • Another app is already bound to ⌥ + M or ⌥ + Space. Open Settings… in Mewt and re-bind to a free combination.
  • macOS hasn't granted Mewt Accessibility access yet (only required if you change the hotkey to a system-reserved combo). Visit System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility if prompted.

Which macOS versions are supported?

macOS 14 (Sonoma) and later.
